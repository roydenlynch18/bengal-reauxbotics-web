About the 3 lbs Robot

Bengal Reauxbotics is dedicated to expanding LSU students’ education experience and interest in robotics through the opportunity to build and compete in robotics competitions, while learning leadership and teamwork skills. Bengal Reauxbotics seeks to provide LSU students with skills vital for engineering education as well as future employment. All who are interested in robotics are welcome regardless of major or experience.
